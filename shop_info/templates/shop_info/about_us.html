About Us

Welcome to our shop! My name is Alba, and I am a mother of two wonderful boys - one and four years old. As a mother, I understand how important it is to have high-quality, safe, and practical products for your little ones. That's why I started this business - to provide parents like you with personalised baby products that not only meet your needs but also reflect your unique style and personality.

I have always had a passion for design and creating things, and this passion only grew stronger when I became a mother. I started making personalised baby products for my own children, and soon enough, family and friends were asking me to make products for their little ones too. That's when I realised that there was a need for personalised baby products that were both practical and stylish.

At our shop, we believe that every child is unique, and their accessories should reflect that. That's why we offer a wide range of customisable products, including pacifier and teether cords, bibs, muslins, and cases to store pacifiers and teethers. All our products are made with love and care, and we only use high-quality, safe materials.

We understand that as a parent, you want the best for your child, and that's what we strive to provide - products that are not only functional but also beautiful and unique. Thank you for choosing our shop, and we look forward to helping you create personalised products that you and your little ones will love.
